welcome to the tree-save app
this app is designed for people with an interest in saving the planet or just are curious about how many trees we are destroying by using vehicles.

the app for asks you for a start point and end point then shows you the time you need for this journey, the emissions and the tree's needed to clean the emissions from the atmosphere.

you can ask for as many journeys as you like if you are curious!
